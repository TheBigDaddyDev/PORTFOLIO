Learning Experience:

Learning web development has been an exhilarating yet challenging journey. Initially, I faced steep learning curves understanding HTML and CSS. The process of coding from scratch was both daunting and rewarding bacause i am used to frameworks and libaries like bootstrap,tailwind and the likes. Ups and downs were part of the journey. Ups included the satisfaction of seeing my code come to life, achieving small milestones, and grasping complex concepts. Downs, on the other hand, involved moments of frustration when bugs seemed insurmountable or design decisions didn't pan out as expected.
